Our Mission

We're here to make a dent in the mental health crisis.

Mental health in America is deteriorating. The numbers aren't abstract - they're people. Our families, our friends, ourselves. We believe the supplement industry has a role to play, and most of it is failing that responsibility.

Our founder knows this firsthand. At 17, Kyle drove an ATV off a cliff at 65 mph. Three brain bleeds. Nine days in the hospital. Years of therapy. But the physical injuries were only half of it. The mental health fallout was worse. Depression. Isolation. No desire to be around people. A version of himself he didn't recognize.

His testosterone came back at sub-200 ng/dL - lower than the average 80-year-old. The healthcare system had nothing for him. No real answers, no real solutions. So he started figuring it out himself - obsessively researching every ingredient, every mechanism, every dosage. Testing everything on his own body for over a decade.

He went from sub-200 to over 800 ng/dL. Without TRT. The depression lifted. The fog cleared. He got his life back - not through the system, but in spite of it.

That's why Formulation Factory exists. Not to sell capsules. To give people the tools to take control of their own biology when the system won't do it for them. Every ingredient published. Every dose transparent. Every batch tested. Because the person on the other end deserves better than what this industry has been giving them.

The supplement industry has a trust problem.

Proprietary blends that hide the fact that key ingredients are underdosed. Brands that claim "third-party tested" but have never published a single COA. Influencers who promote products they've never taken for a check they'll never disclose. Labels designed to look impressive while delivering almost nothing.

Most supplement companies are marketing operations that happen to sell capsules. The product is an afterthought. The formulation is whatever's cheapest. The consumer is treated like someone who won't read the label.

We think that's ending. Consumers are getting smarter. They're reading labels. They're asking for proof. And the brands that can't provide it are going to disappear. We exist because we believe the supplement industry should be held to the same standard as any other industry that puts something in your body. Full transparency. Published testing. Real doses. Real science.
