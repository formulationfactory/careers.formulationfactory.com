Our Culture

The non-negotiables.

Three pillars define who we are, who we hire, and how we build. They aren't aspirational — they're operational.

Character

Radical Honesty

We say what needs to be said — to each other, to our partners, and to our customers. No hidden agendas, no softened truths, no gap between who you are publicly and privately. This extends to how we build our team — we hire people we can be fully honest with and who will be fully honest with us. Integrity here isn't a policy, it's a hiring standard. The people and the brand we're building depend on everyone showing up as exactly who they are, every single day, and holding each other to that same standard.

Execution

Relentless Drive

We know who the best is in every role, every function, and every market we compete in — and we're coming for them. This isn't an effort we bring on good days. It's a deeply held belief that we are building something world class, and that anything short of #1 is unfinished work. We move fast, we execute with urgency, and we treat speed as a competitive advantage — not a risk. We own our outcomes without being asked, and we never accept a ceiling on what this brand can become.

Purpose

Selfless Service

Every formula, every product, every interaction traces back to something bigger than the transaction. An industry built on misleading labels, proprietary blends, and empty promises created the gap we exist to fill. We publish every ingredient, every dose, and every third-party test result because the person on the other end deserves to know exactly what they're putting in their body. We don't just serve the customer — we're driven by them, accountable to them, and committed to earning their trust every single day. They are not the end of our process. They are the reason for it.
