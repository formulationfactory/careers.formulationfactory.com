How We Got Here

It started with a crash.

In 2005, our founder Kyle drove an ATV off a cliff at 65 mph. Three brain bleeds. Nine days in the hospital. Years of therapy. His testosterone came back at sub-200 ng/dL — lower than the average 80-year-old. The healthcare system had nothing for him.

What followed was over a decade of obsessive self-experimentation. Every ingredient. Every dosage. Every mechanism of action — tested on himself first. He went from sub-200 to over 800 ng/dL. Without TRT.

He started giving baggies of his custom blends to friends. They kept coming back. Then their friends came. The turning point came when he saw competitors launching testosterone boosters with underdosed ingredients he'd already tried and discarded years earlier.

Formulation Factory launched in April 2024 and grew to 12 SKUs generating over $13M in annual revenue — by making products that actually work and refusing to cut corners on what goes inside them.
