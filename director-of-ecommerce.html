eCommerce Remote Full-Time

Director of eCommerce

Founder & CEO

Remote (U.S. preferred)

$90K–$140K base + performance bonuses

Mission

Own the Shopify DTC storefront end-to-end — from landing pages to checkout to post-purchase — and turn it from a growing side channel into a high-converting, high-margin revenue engine that scales alongside paid media.

Role Context

Formulation Factory does $13M+ ARR, with ~95% coming through Amazon. DTC is live on Shopify and growing, but it needs an owner. Someone who obsesses over conversion rate, builds landing pages that match the paid creative, wires up email and SMS flows that recover revenue and build LTV, and treats the storefront like a product — always iterating, always improving.

You'll work closely with the Head of Growth (who owns paid media) and the creative team (who produce the ads and UGC). Your job is everything that happens after the click — the landing page, the PDP, the cart, the upsell, the subscription flow, the post-purchase journey. When Growth wants to scale spend, your funnel is what makes that profitable.

Core Accountabilities

1. Storefront and Landing Page Ownership

Own the Shopify storefront: homepage, PDPs, landing pages, collection pages, cart experience, and checkout. You build and ship landing pages for every paid media offer — VSL pages, advertorial pages, quiz funnels, comparison pages, trial offers. You are the bridge between the ad and the conversion. Pixel-level attention to detail, fast turnaround, always optimized for mobile.

2. Conversion Rate Optimization

Own site-wide conversion rate as a primary KPI. Run A/B tests on headlines, layouts, CTAs, pricing presentation, trust elements, and page structure. You use data (GA4, heatmaps, session recordings) to identify drop-off points and fix them systematically. You don't guess — you test, measure, and iterate.

3. Email and SMS (Klaviyo)

Own the full lifecycle email and SMS program. Welcome flows, abandoned cart, browse abandonment, post-purchase education, win-back, subscription reminders, and campaign sends. You build flows that drive measurable revenue and treat Klaviyo as a profit center, not a notification system. Reporting weekly on flow revenue, campaign performance, and list growth.

4. Subscription and LTV

Own the subscription experience — onboarding, retention, churn reduction. Manage the subscription platform (Skio or equivalent), optimize the subscription offer on PDPs, and build post-purchase journeys that increase subscriber LTV. Track and report on subscriber growth, churn rate, and LTV by cohort.

5. Funnel Coordination with Paid Media

Work hand-in-hand with the Head of Growth to ensure every paid media campaign has a matching, optimized landing experience. When a new creative angle wins, you build the corresponding LP within days, not weeks. You maintain ≥ 3 tested, ready-to-scale funnels (offer + LP + post-click experience) so the team can increase spend without conversion rate dropping.

6. DTC Analytics and Reporting

Own a weekly DTC performance report: revenue, MER, conversion rate by funnel, AOV, email/SMS revenue, subscription metrics, and LTV trends. You connect the numbers to decisions — what's working, what's not, and what to do about it.

Explicit Boundaries

This role does not own:

  • Paid media buying or campaign management — that sits with the Head of Growth
  • Amazon marketplace — that's the Head of Amazon
  • Brand positioning or product philosophy — those belong to the Founder
  • UGC or ad creative production — you brief what you need, the creative team builds it

You own everything between the ad click and the repeat purchase. The storefront, the conversion, the retention.

Decision Rights

Owns: Shopify storefront, all landing pages, email/SMS strategy and execution, subscription experience, CRO testing, and DTC performance reporting.

Reports to: Founder & CEO.

Collaborates with: Head of Growth on paid media ↔ landing page alignment, Creative team on assets and UGC, Head of Operations on fulfillment and subscription logistics, and CX Lead on post-purchase experience.

This Role Is Not
  • A Shopify developer who waits for tickets — you own the strategy and the execution
  • A generalist marketer with ecommerce as one of five responsibilities
  • An email manager who sends newsletters — you build a revenue engine
  • A designer who makes things look nice but doesn't track conversion rate
Ideal Candidate
  • 3–6 years owning a Shopify DTC storefront at a brand doing $2M–$20M+ in DTC revenue
  • Deep Shopify expertise — Liquid, theme customization, app ecosystem (Klaviyo, Skio/ReCharge, Rebuy, Okendo, Shogun or equivalent)
  • Proven CRO chops — you've run tests, improved conversion rates, and can talk through your methodology
  • Built and managed Klaviyo flows that drove measurable revenue, not just opens and clicks
  • Supplement, health, wellness, or fitness brand experience strongly preferred
  • Can build and ship landing pages fast — you don't need a developer for every change
  • Data-driven — you live in GA4, Shopify analytics, and Klaviyo dashboards and make decisions from them
