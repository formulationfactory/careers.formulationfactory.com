Chief Operating Officer

Founder & CEO

Remote (U.S. preferred, must overlap AST)

$150K–$250K base + equity & performance incentives

Mission

Own day-to-day execution across every function at Formulation Factory — growth, operations, Amazon, DTC, finance, supply chain, and team — so the Founder is freed to focus on acquisitions, major partnerships, content, and long-term brand vision. Scale the business from $13M to $20M+ in year one, in partnership with the Founder and channel leads, without breaking what's working or burning out the team.

Role Context

This role exists because the business has outgrown what one person can run. We are at $13M+ ARR and scaling hard. You are the Founder's co-pilot — not a traditional executive who manages from altitude, but a genuine operator who thinks like a founder and moves like an athlete.

You are biased toward speed and decisive action, but you know how to protect the team from chaos with clear priorities and simple systems. You are not here to sit above the work. You are here to be in it, alongside the CEO, at the same intensity.

If you have a strong opinion about magnesium glycinate vs threonate, know what your cortisol looks like at noon, and have built or scaled something real in the DTC supplement space — keep reading.

Scorecard — Outcomes

This is how we measure success. Not effort, not hours, not how busy you look — outcomes with metrics and deadlines.

By Month 3
  • The Founder spends <5 hrs/week on recurring ops and channel meetings (Amazon, DTC, CX, supply chain)
  • A single weekly operating cadence is in place — scorecard, issues list, clear owner per metric — and is followed 4 out of 4 weeks by all leaders
By Month 6
  • Amazon: Hero SKU in-stock rate ≥ 98%. TACOS improved by at least 3–5 pts vs baseline while maintaining or increasing total Amazon revenue. Weekly dashboard live with revenue, TACOS, ACOS, CVR, and contribution margin per order
  • DTC: MER improved by at least 20% vs baseline, or DTC revenue doubled at same MER. Core funnel and email/SMS flows are defined, documented, and reported weekly
  • TikTok & Meta: Always maintain ≥ 3 tested, ready-to-scale funnels (offer + LP + ad angles) per channel, with playbooks documented so we can increase spend 2–3x without new ops or CX bottlenecks
By Month 9
  • Company topline run rate is up 30%+ vs prior year with contribution margin equal or higher than at hire date
  • A rolling 13-week cash forecast is updated weekly and is within ±10% variance of actuals for at least 3 consecutive months
  • At least 3 major workflows (e.g., reporting, CX macros, purchase order process, content briefs) are automated or AI-assisted, reducing manual hours by ≥30% without adding headcount
By Month 12
  • Company revenue has grown 40%+ year-on-year while maintaining or improving contribution margin — north of $20M and the trajectory is accelerating, not plateauing
  • DTC is ≥ 15% of total revenue (up from ~5% at hire) with profitable unit economics and MER within range of Amazon's contribution margin
  • Every team member has a written scorecard (3–5 KPIs each) and a weekly 1:1 rhythm with their manager
  • 100% of B- and C-players identified: either improved to hitting their scorecard for 3 consecutive months, or exited and replaced
  • No single point of failure: each core function (Amazon, DTC, CX, Supply Chain) has a clear owner, documented SOPs, and at least 1 trained backup
  • The Founder spends <2 hrs/week on ops. Their calendar is majority acquisitions, partnerships, content, and long-term brand strategy

Explicit Boundaries

This role does not own:

  • Product vision, core formulation philosophy, or brand worldview — those belong to the Founder
  • Final authority on brand standards or long-term mission — you execute within them and challenge constructively when needed
  • Creative direction — you ensure creative output is resourced and on schedule, not that you set the creative vision

The brand's non-negotiable standards — ingredient transparency, third-party testing, no misleading marketing claims — are the foundation you operate on.

Decision Rights

Owns: All execution-level decisions, resource allocation, operational priorities, team performance management, and channel-level strategy within guardrails set by the Founder.

Reports to: Founder & CEO.

Collaborates with: Head of Growth on paid media and DTC, Head of Amazon on marketplace execution, Head of Operations on supply chain and 3PL, and all agency partners.

This Role Is Not
  • A traditional COO who manages from a dashboard and delegates everything downward
  • A generalist operator who needs to be educated on the supplement or DTC ecommerce space
  • A cautious executive who slows things down to build consensus
  • A reactive tornado — all gas, no steering wheel
Ideal Candidate
  • 5+ years operating in DTC ecommerce with experience managing teams across Amazon FBA, Shopify, and paid social
  • Supplement, health, wellness, or fitness industry experience strongly preferred
  • A genuine, personal obsession with health optimization — you track your bloodwork, you know your stack, you follow the science
  • Strong financial literacy — you can own a P&L, build a forecast, and connect operational decisions to financial outcomes
  • You have built or scaled a brand, a team, or a business unit from something small to something real

Bonus: You've built custom GPTs, automation workflows, or AI-powered systems that meaningfully changed how a team operates.
