TikTok Brand Manager

This is a channel GM role, not a content creator role. You are responsible for revenue generated through TikTok, not just followers or views.

Head of Growth & Founder

Remote (U.S. strongly preferred)

$100K+ base + commission on TikTok Shop GMV

Role Purpose

The TikTok Brand Manager owns Formulation Factory's entire TikTok ecosystem — organic channel, TikTok Shop, paid dark posts, and the affiliate and creator network that feeds all three.

You will build and manage a high-output affiliate and creator program, oversee content strategy and publishing cadence on the organic channel, coordinate with our paid media team on whitelisting and dark post amplification, and own the TikTok Shop infrastructure from product listing to conversion optimization.

We are not looking for someone who understands TikTok in theory. We are looking for someone who has built something real on it — a channel, a shop, an affiliate program, or ideally all three — and is ready to do it again at a brand with the product quality and creative infrastructure to support serious scale.

Core Accountabilities

1. TikTok Channel Ownership

Own the Formulation Factory TikTok channel end to end. Set and execute the organic content strategy, maintain a consistent publishing cadence, brief and oversee content creation in collaboration with the Creative Strategist, and ensure the channel reflects the brand's voice with accuracy and authority. You are accountable for channel growth, engagement benchmarks, and the quality of every piece of content that goes live.

2. Affiliate and Creator Program

Build, manage, and scale a high-performing TikTok affiliate and creator network from the ground up. This includes recruiting and vetting creators, negotiating terms, briefing content, tracking performance by creator and by post, managing payouts, and continuously optimizing the roster based on ROI. You know how to find creators who actually convert — not just creators with reach — and you have the systems to manage a large network without it becoming chaotic.

3. TikTok Shop Management

Own the TikTok Shop infrastructure: product listings, pricing strategy, promotional setup, shipping and fulfillment coordination with Operations, and ongoing conversion rate optimization within the platform. You understand how the Shop algorithm works, how affiliate commissions interact with organic and paid traffic, and how to run Shop-specific campaigns that drive measurable GMV. Revenue through TikTok Shop is a core KPI for this role.

4. Paid Media Coordination

Work closely with the Head of Growth and paid media agencies to identify top-performing organic and creator content for whitelisting and dark post amplification. You brief the paid team on winning hooks, angles, and formats and ensure there is a continuous pipeline of creator-approved content available for amplification.

5. Performance Reporting and Revenue Accountability

Own a weekly TikTok performance report covering channel growth, video performance, affiliate GMV by creator, TikTok Shop revenue, and commission spend vs. revenue generated. You track every dollar in and out of the TikTok ecosystem and use that data to make fast decisions on creator roster management, content doubling, and budget allocation. This role has a commission component tied directly to the revenue it drives.

6. Brand and Compliance Integrity

TikTok has specific rules around supplement advertising and health claims. You stay ahead of platform policy, ensure every piece of content complies with FTC disclosure requirements and TikTok's advertising guidelines, and protect the brand from enforcement risk. A viral video with a bad health claim is a liability, not a win.

Explicit Boundaries

This role does not own:

  • Paid media buying or campaign management on TikTok Ads Manager — that sits with the Head of Growth and agency partners
  • Brand positioning or product philosophy — those decisions belong to the Founder
  • Creative strategy for other channels — your creative ownership is TikTok-specific
  • Influencer relationships outside of TikTok
  • Final pricing or margin decisions on TikTok Shop — you propose, Operations and Finance approve
Decision Rights & Key Interfaces

Owns: TikTok channel strategy and publishing, affiliate and creator program, TikTok Shop operations, creator content briefing and approval, and all TikTok-specific performance reporting.

Reports to: Head of Growth for channel performance and revenue alignment; Founder for brand voice and product accuracy questions.

Collaborates closely with: Creative Strategist on organic and creator briefs, Paid Media team on whitelisting and dark post pipeline, Operations on TikTok Shop fulfillment, Finance on affiliate commission tracking, and CX Lead on community escalations.

This Role Is Not
  • A content creator who posts videos and hopes they go viral
  • A social media coordinator managing a content calendar for one of several platforms
  • An influencer manager who sends PR packages and tracks impressions
  • A paid media manager who happens to work on TikTok

This is a channel general manager who owns revenue, builds systems, and treats TikTok as a business unit — not a content channel with a commission structure bolted on.

Year-One Success Profile
  • A functioning TikTok affiliate and creator program is live with a roster of active, revenue-generating creators
  • TikTok Shop is generating meaningful GMV with a clear optimization roadmap
  • The organic channel is publishing consistently and growing followers and engagement quarter over quarter
  • A whitelisting pipeline is feeding the paid media team a steady stream of creator content
  • Commission spend is tracked against GMV with clean attribution and improving efficiency
  • TikTok is an established, measurable revenue channel for the brand — not an experiment
Ideal Candidate
  • 3–5+ years of hands-on TikTok experience — you have built or scaled a TikTok channel, a TikTok Shop, or a creator and affiliate program and have the results to show for it
  • Deep knowledge of TikTok Shop: how the algorithm works, how affiliate commissions are structured, how to optimize listings and promotions for conversion
  • Proven affiliate and creator program experience — you have recruited, managed, and optimized a roster of creators at scale, not just run a gifting program
  • Experience in the supplement, health, fitness, wellness, or CPG space strongly preferred
  • Commercially minded — you track GMV, commission efficiency, CAC, and ROI, not just views and follower counts
  • Familiarity with FTC affiliate disclosure requirements and TikTok's supplement advertising policies
Compensation Structure

This role carries a base salary of $100,000+ with a performance commission tied directly to TikTok Shop GMV and affiliate-driven revenue. The commission structure is designed to reward meaningful scale — the expectation is that a high performer in this role earns significantly above base. Specific commission tiers will be discussed during the interview process.
