TikTok Brand Manager

Last updated: April 2026

Director of eCommerce

Remote (U.S. only)

$110K base + performance bonus

Mission

Own the entire TikTok ecosystem at Formulation Factory - organic channel, TikTok Shop, and the affiliate and creator network that feeds both - and build it from near-zero into a meaningful, measurable revenue channel.

Role Context

TikTok is currently not set up. No active Shop, no affiliate program, no consistent organic presence. This is a greenfield build. You are building the channel, the infrastructure, and the revenue from scratch.

This is a channel GM role, not a content creator role. You are responsible for revenue generated through TikTok, not just followers or views. You will build and manage a high-output affiliate and creator program, oversee organic content strategy and publishing cadence, coordinate with paid media on whitelisting and dark post amplification, and own the TikTok Shop from product listing to conversion optimization.

We are not looking for someone who understands TikTok in theory. We are looking for someone who has built something real on it - a channel, a shop, an affiliate program, or ideally all three - and is ready to do it again at a brand with the product quality and creative infrastructure to support serious scale.

Scorecard - Outcomes

This is how we measure success. Not effort, not hours - outcomes with metrics and deadlines.

By Day 30
  • TikTok ecosystem audit complete: assessed current state of account, any existing content, competitor presence on TikTok, and the creator/affiliate landscape in the supplement category. Written report delivered to Director of eCommerce.
  • TikTok Shop setup initiated: account verified, product listings in progress, pricing strategy drafted, shipping and fulfillment coordination started with operations. Any compliance or platform policy blockers identified and escalated.
  • Creator and affiliate strategy defined: target creator profiles identified, commission structure drafted, outreach templates built, and a tracking system in place. First batch of creator outreach launched.
  • Content strategy documented: organic content pillars defined (educational, authority, UGC, trending formats). Publishing cadence set. Working with creative team on first round of content briefs.
  • Competitive landscape mapped: top 10 supplement brands on TikTok analyzed - what's working, what formats drive engagement, what creator strategies they use, and where we can differentiate.
By Day 60
  • TikTok Shop live: products listed, Shop is active, first sales coming through. Promotional setup and initial campaigns running. GMV tracked from day one.
  • First creators onboarded: at least 10-15 creators vetted, contracted, and briefed. First creator content published. Performance tracked by creator and by post.
  • Organic channel publishing consistently: content going out on a regular cadence. Mix of branded content, creator reposts, educational content, and trend-based content. Engagement growing.
  • Whitelisting pipeline started: top-performing organic and creator content identified and shared with paid media team for dark post amplification. Process documented.
  • Weekly TikTok report live: covering channel growth, video performance, Shop GMV, creator performance, and commission spend. Reviewed in operating meeting.
By Day 90
  • TikTok Shop generating consistent GMV: not a one-time spike - repeatable revenue with clear attribution. Conversion rate optimization underway on listings.
  • Creator roster scaled: 25+ active creators in the program. Performance-based roster management in place - doubling down on top performers, cutting underperformers. Commission efficiency tracked.
  • Organic channel growing: follower count and engagement rate improving week over week. Content performance data informing what gets made next.
  • Affiliate program systematized: creator recruitment, briefing, content approval, performance tracking, and payout management running as a repeatable system, not ad hoc.
  • Compliance clean: every piece of content complies with FTC disclosure requirements and TikTok's supplement advertising guidelines. Zero enforcement actions or content takedowns due to compliance failures.
By Month 6
  • TikTok is a real revenue channel: not an experiment. TikTok Shop is generating meaningful monthly GMV with a clear optimization roadmap. Revenue is growing month over month.
  • Creator program is a machine: 50+ active creators, commission spend tracked against GMV with improving efficiency. A continuous pipeline of new creators being onboarded as underperformers are rotated out.
  • Whitelisting pipeline feeding paid at scale: a steady stream of creator-approved content available for amplification. Paid team never starved for TikTok creative.
  • Organic presence established: consistent publishing, growing audience, engagement above supplement category benchmarks. Channel reflects the brand voice with accuracy and authority.
  • Full TikTok P&L visibility: every dollar in and out of the TikTok ecosystem tracked - Shop revenue, commission spend, ad amplification ROI, organic attribution. You can answer "is TikTok profitable?" with a number, not a feeling.
Ongoing KPIs

Once the system is built, these are the numbers you own and review weekly. This is your scoreboard.

  • TikTok Shop GMV: monthly gross merchandise value. The primary revenue metric for this role.
  • Commission efficiency: commission spend as % of affiliate-attributed GMV. Target improving over time.
  • Active creator count: number of creators actively posting in the current month. Target: 50+ by month 6.
  • Creator-attributed GMV: revenue generated through affiliate links and creator content. Tracked per creator.
  • Organic follower growth: net new followers per week/month on the brand TikTok account.
  • Organic engagement rate: likes, comments, shares, saves relative to views. Target: above supplement category benchmarks.
  • Publishing cadence: posts per week on organic channel. Consistent output with no gaps.
  • Whitelisting pipeline volume: number of creator-approved assets available for paid amplification per week.
  • Shop conversion rate: TikTok Shop listing views to purchase. Optimized through listing quality, pricing, and promotions.
  • Compliance record: zero FTC violations, zero content takedowns due to health claim or disclosure failures.

Explicit Boundaries

This role does not own:

  • Paid media buying or campaign management on TikTok Ads Manager - that sits with the paid media function and agency partners
  • Product vision, formulation philosophy, or brand worldview - those belong to the Founder
  • Creative strategy for other channels - your creative ownership is TikTok-specific
  • Final pricing or margin decisions on TikTok Shop - you propose, operations and finance approve

You own everything TikTok. The organic channel, the Shop, the creator network, and the content pipeline that feeds all three. When the paid team needs TikTok creative to amplify, you're the one supplying it.

Decision Rights

Owns: TikTok channel strategy and publishing, TikTok Shop operations (listings, pricing proposals, promotions, CRO), affiliate and creator program (recruitment, briefing, approval, tracking, payouts), whitelisting pipeline, and all TikTok-specific performance reporting.

Reports to: Director of eCommerce.

Collaborates with: Creative team on organic and creator content briefs, paid media on whitelisting and dark post pipeline, operations on TikTok Shop fulfillment, finance on commission tracking and payouts, and CX on community escalations.

This Role Is Not
  • A content creator who posts videos and hopes they go viral
  • A social media coordinator managing a calendar for one of several platforms
  • An influencer manager who sends PR packages and tracks impressions
  • A paid media manager who happens to work on TikTok

This is a channel general manager who owns revenue, builds systems, and treats TikTok as a business unit - not a content channel with a commission structure bolted on.

Ideal Candidate
  • 3-5+ years of hands-on TikTok experience - you have built or scaled a TikTok channel, a TikTok Shop, or a creator/affiliate program and have the results to show for it
  • Deep knowledge of TikTok Shop: how the algorithm works, how affiliate commissions are structured, how to optimize listings and promotions for conversion
  • Proven affiliate and creator program experience - you have recruited, managed, and optimized a roster of creators at scale, not just run a gifting program
  • Supplement, health, fitness, wellness, or CPG experience strongly preferred
  • Commercially minded - you track GMV, commission efficiency, CAC, and ROI, not just views and follower counts
  • Familiarity with FTC affiliate disclosure requirements and TikTok's supplement advertising policies
  • Proficient with AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) as daily workflow tools
  • U.S.-based - this is a U.S.-only hire

Bonus: You've taken a brand's TikTok presence from zero to a real revenue channel. You've built the Shop, the creator program, and the organic presence from scratch.
